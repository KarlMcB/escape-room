📋 CSV Format Guide

Your CSV needs two types of rows: stage rows and question rows.

Stage rows:
type, stage, stage_image_url, grid_cols, grid_rows, designator_type
stage, 1, https://img.url/bg.jpg, 8, 6, letters
Question rows:
type, stage, question, question_text, question_image_url, correct_answer(1-6), a1_text, a1_image, a2_text, a2_image, …a6, object_name, object_col, object_row
question, 1, 1, What is the capital?, , 2, Berlin, , Paris, , Rome, , , , , , , , Eiffel Tower, 3, 2

Designator types: letters · numbers · emoji · symbols · roman · greek
